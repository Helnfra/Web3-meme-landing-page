About

Founded in 2024 by Kamis, Purr & Play began as a heartfelt project fueled by a deep passion for cats. What started as a personal endeavor to enhance the lives of our own feline friends has grown into a vibrant business.

From humble beginnings, Purr & Play has blossomed into a trusted name in the cat community. Today, we proudly offer an extensive range of carefully curated products and services, all designed with the well-being of both cats and their owners in mind.

At Purr & Play, our mission is simple: to provide cat lovers with high-quality products and services that enhance the lives of their furry companions. We believe that every cat deserves to be pampered and every owner deserves to enjoy a harmonious relationship with their feline friend.

We are excited to continue growing and sharing our passion with you, ensuring that every cat receives the care, comfort, and joy they deserve.
